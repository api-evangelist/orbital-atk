# Orbital ATK (orbital-atk)
Orbital ATK was an American aerospace manufacturer and defense industry company formed in February 2015 by the merger of Orbital Sciences Corporation and the defense and aerospace divisions of Alliant Techsystems. The company produced launch vehicles, satellites, missiles, ammunition, precision-guided munitions, rocket motors, and the Cygnus spacecraft for ISS resupply before being acquired by Northrop Grumman on June 6, 2018 and rebranded as Northrop Grumman Innovation Systems.
